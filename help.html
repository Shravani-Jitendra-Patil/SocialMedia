Talkzi Help Center

Welcome to the Talkzi Help Center

Talkzi is a user-friendly social media web application designed to connect people and facilitate engaging conversations. This page provides essential information to help you navigate and make the most out of Talkzi.

Getting Started

To start using Talkzi, simply create an account by registering on our main page. Once registered, log in to access your dashboard where you can create posts, interact with other users, and manage your profile.

Features Overview

Talkzi includes the following core features:

  • Posting: Share updates, stories, and multimedia content.
  • Comments: Engage with posts through comments and discussions.
  • Profile Management: Customize your profile with your photo and bio.
  • Notifications: Stay informed with real-time notifications about new activity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: Can I delete my account?

A: Yes, go to your account settings and select the "Delete Account" option. Be aware that this action is irreversible.
